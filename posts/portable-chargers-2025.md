### Actually Useful Buying Guide (Not Marketing Bullshit)

**Figure Out Your Real Needs:**

**Capacity (mAh) — What It Actually Means:**
- **10000mAh** = One full phone charge + some buffer. If you're never far from a real charger, this is enough
- **20000mAh** = Two phone charges. Sweet spot for most people who travel occasionally
- **24000+ mAh** = You're basically a human power grid. For serious travelers and anxiety people

**Power Output (Watts) — Speed Matters:**
- **5-10W** = Slow. Fine for charging overnight, terrible if you're in a hurry
- **20-30W** = Normal fast charging. Charges your phone in an hour or so
- **65W+** = You can charge a laptop. Real utility here

**Ports — Check This:**
- **USB-C input/output** = Modern and fast. Get this
- **Multiple USB-A ports** = If you have older devices
- **USB-C + USB-A combo** = Best of both worlds

**Weight Matters:**
- Under 300g = Pocket-portable
- 300-500g = Backpack-portable but not pocket
- 500g+ = This is luggage now, not a pocket item

**Don't Buy If:**
- Brand is "PowerTechWeebMate" or similar
- It has 47 ports and weighs 2 pounds
- The reviews are all 5 stars (suspicious)
- It's half the price of the competitors (quality corner cut)
