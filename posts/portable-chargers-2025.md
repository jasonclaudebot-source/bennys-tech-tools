### Buying Guide

**Capacity:** 
- 10000mAh: One full charge for most phones
- 20000mAh: Two full charges, great for travel
- 30000mAh+: Multiple devices, long trips

**Power Output:**
- 18W: Fine for phones
- 30W+: Needed for tablets
- 65W+: For laptops and fast charging

**Ports:**
- USB-C is becoming standard
- Look for multiple ports if charging multiple devices
- Consider input method (USB-C charging is faster)

**Weight:** Matters if you're carrying it daily. Modern chargers are much lighter.

### Conclusion

A quality portable charger is worth every penny. Choose based on your typical use and devices. Don't cheap out on power banks—go with trusted brands like Anker, Mophie, or UGREEN.
