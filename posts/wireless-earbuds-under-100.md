### What Actually Matters When You're Shopping

Don't get fooled by marketing nonsense. Here's what you *actually* need:

- **Battery Life:** Minimum 6 hours before you need the case. Less than that and they're basically paperweights
- **Noise Cancellation:** Even cheap ANC is better than nothing. Real talk
- **Sound Quality:** Try them out if you can. Bass-heavy isn't always better — depends on what you listen to
- **Comfort:** This is the one thing companies still mess up. Multiple ear tip sizes is non-negotiable
- **Durability:** Will these survive being dropped? Probably. Will they survive a washing machine? Let's not test it
