### What to Look For

When shopping for budget earbuds, prioritize:
- **Battery Life:** Look for at least 6 hours per charge
- **Noise Cancellation:** Even basic ANC makes a difference
- **Sound Quality:** Test the bass and midrange
- **Comfort:** Lightweight design with multiple ear tips
