### Getting Started

**Pick a Hub:** Choose Alexa, Google Home, or Apple HomeKit as your primary ecosystem.

**Start Small:** Begin with 2-3 devices and expand from there.

**Prioritize:** Security (cameras, locks) then comfort (lights, thermostat).
