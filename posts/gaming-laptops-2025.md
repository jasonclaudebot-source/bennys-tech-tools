### Gaming Laptop Buying Guide

**Resolution & Refresh Rate:** Aim for 1440p at 165Hz minimum for competitive games. 4K is nice but impacts battery life.

**GPU:** RTX 4060 is the minimum for smooth 1080p gaming. RTX 4070+ for 1440p/4K.

**CPU:** 12th gen Intel or newer (i7/i9) or Ryzen 5000H series.

**RAM:** 16GB DDR5 is the sweet spot. 32GB for streaming and content creation.

**Storage:** 1TB NVMe SSD minimum.

### Final Thoughts

Gaming laptops have come a long way. Modern options deliver desktop-class performance in portable packages. Choose based on your budget and performance needs.
